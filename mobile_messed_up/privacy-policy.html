Privacy Policy

TPS Connect — School Communication Platform

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Twinkling Petals School ("we," "us," or "our") operates the TPS Connect mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By downloading, installing, or using TPS Connect, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and role (parent, teacher, or administrator) provided during account creation or login.
  • Profile Information: Information associated with your user profile such as your assigned branch, linked students (for parents), or class assignments (for teachers).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and push notification tokens (Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens).
  • Usage Data: App interaction data such as features accessed, timestamps, and session information.
  • Authentication Data: Firebase Authentication tokens used to verify your identity and maintain secure sessions.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Phone Authentication: If you sign in via phone OTP, we process your phone number through Firebase Authentication.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication & Security: To verify your identity and provide secure access to the App.
  • Communication: To send push notifications about school announcements, attendance updates, homework, and important notices.
  • App Functionality: To display relevant dashboards, attendance records, posts, and other features based on your role.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve App performance and user experience.
  • Administrative Operations: To enable school administrators to manage branches, users, and communications efficiently.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within the School Network: Your information may be visible to authorized school administrators and teachers within your assigned branch as necessary for school operations.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, cloud messaging, and backend services. Google processes data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Render (backend hosting) and Firebase (Google Cloud Platform).
  • All data transmission between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are securely managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you wish to have your data removed, please contact the school administration. Upon account deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

TPS Connect is designed for use by parents, teachers, and school administrators. The App does not directly collect information from children under 13. Student information (such as names and attendance records) is managed by authorized school staff and parents. We comply with applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA guidelines.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications through your device settings at any time.

9. Third-Party Services

TPS Connect integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — User sign-in and identity verification
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — Push notification delivery
  • Google Sign-In — Optional social login

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Firebase's Privacy and Security documentation.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App or via push notification. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
